Product Description

Main functions and features:

1. Adopt a new generation of 7-inch color touch screen, colorful, touch control, good man-machine control interface.
2. Based on the single-chip microcomputer program independently developed by the ImageCraftCB 8.26 program, the modular multi-level function menu is convenient and simple to operate, and the required functions and additional functions of the pillow packaging machine are realized.
3.8 kinds of commonly used product application parameters are stored and called at any time when changing product specifications.
4. High-speed operation of the system: with the use of servo motor system to pull the film, the maximum packaging speed of 1200 packs/min.
5. Color touch screen and control module integrated design, simple structure, less failure.
6. Chinese, English, Arabic, Russian and other multi-language operation interface conversion function, applicable to the international market.
7. SMT chip production process, more stable performance.
